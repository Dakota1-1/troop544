Who We Are

More Than Scouting — A Lifelong Brotherhood

For over 67 years, Troop 544 has been shaping the young men of New Hyde Park into confident leaders, skilled adventurers, and compassionate community members through the Boy Scouts of America program.

  • Weekly meetings every Wednesday, 7:30–9:00 PM
  • Monthly camping trips across the region
  • Full rank advancement from Scout to Eagle
  • Open to all boys — come visit any Wednesday!

What We Do

Adventure at Every Turn

From the trails to the classroom, Troop 544 builds well-rounded young men ready for anything life throws their way.

Outdoor Adventures

Monthly camping trips, hiking expeditions, and District Camporees build resilience, navigation skills, and a lasting love for the outdoors.

Merit Badge Program

Over 100 merit badges spanning STEM, arts, citizenship, and more — every badge is a new skill and a step toward Eagle Scout.

Leadership Development

Scouts take on patrol leadership roles, plan events, and mentor younger members — building the confidence to lead in every area of life.

Community Service

From local park cleanups to Eagle Scout service projects, Troop 544 gives back to the New Hyde Park community in a meaningful way.
