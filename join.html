Why Troop 544?

What Makes Our Troop Special

67 Years of History

Since 1958, Troop 544 has been a trusted institution in New Hyde Park. Generations of leaders started here.

Monthly Camping

We go on a camping trip every month — real outdoor adventure with trained leaders and a supportive team of scouts.

Brotherhood

The friendships built in Troop 544 last a lifetime. Our scouts become each other's greatest supporters on and off the trail.

Eagle Scout Path

We have the program, the mentorship, and the commitment to help every scout who wants it reach Eagle Scout.

Getting Started

Three Simple Steps

1

Reach Out

Email us at t544t544@gmail.com or use the contact form below. We'll answer your questions and invite you to a meeting.

2

Visit a Meeting

Come to any Wednesday meeting from 7:30–9:00 PM at Manor Oaks School. Bring a parent. There's no commitment — just come and see.

3

Join the Troop

If it's a fit, complete BSA registration and start your scouting journey with Troop 544. Your first rank is already within reach.

First Visit

What to Expect at Your First Meeting

Walking into a troop meeting for the first time can feel a bit daunting — but Troop 544 scouts and leaders make every visitor feel welcome from the moment they arrive.

  • Arrive at Manor Oaks School by 7:30 PM
  • You'll be introduced to the Scoutmaster and patrol leaders
  • Observe a regular meeting in progress
  • Ask questions — leaders are happy to help
  • No uniform required on your first visit
  • Parents are welcome to stay and watch

Eligibility

Who Can Join?

  • → Boys who have completed 5th grade, or who are 11 years old (whichever comes first)
  • → Any boy under 18 years of age who has not yet earned Eagle Scout
  • → Transfer scouts from other troops are welcome
  • → No prior scouting experience required
